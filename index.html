Research Interest

From early civilization, humans and plants are closely associated as plants provide shelter, medicine, and above all oxygen. I gain interest in traditional medicines and natural products because these are still important sources of drugs and lead compounds. However, we are investigating pharmacological activity, computational analysis, and toxicological study of some traditional medicines as well as some indigenous plants on the animal model (rats, mice). We intend to identify new therapeutics from natural sources that are devoid of toxicity.
